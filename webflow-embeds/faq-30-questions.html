What is an AI receptionist?
An AI receptionist is a virtual assistant that answers your business calls 24/7 using natural language processing. An auto-attendant (IVR - Interactive Voice Response) greets callers with a menu: 'Press 1 for Sales, 2 for Support.' SMB Voice's AI receptionist goes beyond basic IVR by understanding natural speech, so callers can say what they need instead of pressing buttons.

What is a virtual phone number?
A virtual phone number is a telephone number not tied to a specific phone line or device. It works over the internet (VoIP) and can ring on any device - smartphone, tablet, computer, or desk phone. SMB Voice virtual numbers include local or toll-free options starting at $7.95/month.
What is the difference between local and toll-free numbers?
Local numbers have area codes specific to geographic regions (like 212 for NYC), making your business appear local to customers. Toll-free numbers (800, 888, 877, 866, 855, 844, 833) are free for callers and project a national presence. SMB Voice offers both at the same $7.95/month price.

Is SMB Voice VoIP or landline?
SMB Voice is VoIP (Voice over Internet Protocol), meaning calls travel over the internet rather than traditional phone lines. This enables lower costs, more features, and device flexibility. VoIP quality equals or exceeds landline quality with a good internet connection.
What internet speed do I need for VoIP?
SMB Voice requires minimal bandwidth - about 100 Kbps per call. Most home and business internet connections easily support this. For best quality, we recommend at least 1 Mbps upload speed. WiFi, cellular data (4G/5G), and wired ethernet all work well.
